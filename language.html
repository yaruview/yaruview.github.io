Interested in learning Chinese language? There are several places you can go in Gainesville.

Hua-Gen Chinese School

It is a good place for children to learn Chinese in this school. If you want your children to learn Chinese, Hua-Gen is the right place. It has Chinese classes from first grade to 10th grade. It also has a Happy Chinese class for children who have no previous involvement in Chinese environment. If you are an adult, you can also contact the school because there are many teachers in the school can help you to learn Chinese.

Besides Chinese classes, Hua-Gen Chinese school also provides leisure courses for students to get emerged in Chinese culture. Right now, the school opens courses on martial arts, Chinese instruments (Gu Zheng), and Chinese dancing. There are both children classes and adults classes.

Want to know more? See Hua-Gen website.

Chinese programs at UF & Santa Fe College

If you are student who want to pursue your degree in Chinese, then Chinese programs at UF or Santa Fe College might be your interest.
